Have you seen the schedule?!

Submitted by kelly on Wed, 09/10/2014 - 4:32am

News Type: 
Announcements

Level up your skills on Friday, be amazed by this industry on Saturday, and learn how anyone can contribute back to it on Sunday!

Friday

This day includes training sessions on Design, Drupal, Joomla! and WordPress, as well as fundamentals in HTML and CSS up to Python/Django for experienced web developers. Learn Node.js in a 3 hour Nodeschool session or firm up your understanding of how DNS works. Be sure to check out the Job Fair and explore what careers exist in this industry. Friday ends with a keynote by Susan Buck, co-founder of the Women's Coding Collective. 

If you are a beginner wanting nail down some basics or are an experienced programmer wanting to branch out, Friday is not to be missed! Check out the fullFriday schedule to see this and more!

Saturday

Saturday starts off strong with a keynote from Ashe Dryden and continues with presentations on Drupal, WordPress, UX, Content Strategy, Performance and more!

Holding true to being a summit, we've gathered industry experts together to discussDiversifying IT, the Business of the Web, and Large Scale Web Infrastructure. Don't miss these discussions and come with your questions!

The highlight of the day may, in fact, be the Youth track, covering intros to the web,programming, MIT's Scratch for Kids and Educators, and Lego WeDO.

End the night with Summit conclusions, Q&A, and an evening mixer with NERDSummit organizers, followed by optional local night life activities.

Sunday

This is the day where everything comes together. All skill levels come together to collaborate on software projects. See what you've learned over the weekend in action! Start with a keynote from Damien McKenna on contributing to open source projects, continue on to get started contributing to Drupal core or check out the Hack for Change or MakerSpace projects available. Enjoy the last session of the weekend on "Shaping Your Free Software Project... and the World" or continue working on your project until we come together one more time to conclude the 2014 NERDSummit.

The NERDSummit, this September 12-14 at UMASS/Amherst, is this weekend!
