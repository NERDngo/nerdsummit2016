Program

Our Summit is scheduled for the 11th-13th September 2015 at UMass Hotel and Conference Center, Amherst, MA 01003 with lodging available at the UMass Hotel.

Please note that once you arrive on UMass Campus, the building in which the Conference Center and Hotel are located may be referred to on signage as the Murray D. Lincoln Campus Center.

Parking on campus is free on Saturdays and Sundays, and we will have vouchers that cap parking in the Garage (located underground adjacent to the Conference Center) at $5 per day. The Garage is not free to park on weekends, but requires the least walking to the event space.

NERD Summit is an expansion of the traditional learning camp and its intended audience. Running on a larger scal than typical camps, the weekend will include Drupal, Wordpress, Beginner Intros, a Job Fair, Panel Discussions, BOFs and Makerspace. Sponsors will be present throughout the weekend.  

The following is the approximate schedule of events across the weekend. We will add details as they become available.
